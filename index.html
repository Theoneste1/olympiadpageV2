About RwMO

The Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad (RwMO) fosters mathematical excellence by identifying and nurturing talent among students across the country. It enhances problem-solving skills, inspires STEM careers, and improves mathematics education. RwMO offers opportunities for students to compete nationally and internationally, building a sense of community and promoting collaboration. By nurturing future leaders in STEM, RwMO contributes to Rwanda's development. Committed to inclusivity, RwMO ensures all students have the opportunity to participate, promoting equal opportunity and discovering hidden talents. Join RwMO to empower students, strengthen education, and contribute to Rwanda's growth in STEM fields.

Our Vision

The vision of the Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad is to foster a culture of mathematical excellence and innovation among Rwandan students. It aims to empower them to become leaders in STEM fields and make meaningful contributions to societal advancement.

Our Mission

The Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad's mission is to identify and nurture mathematical talent among Rwandan students through engaging competitions and training programs. We promote problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity, inspiring a passion for mathematics and STEM education. Ultimately, we aim to develop Rwanda's future STEM leaders, fostering a culture of excellence and innovation in the nation.

Our Value

At the Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad, we uphold values of excellence, equity, innovation, and collaboration. Our commitment to integrity, diversity, and continuous improvement guides our actions and decisions. We strive to provide equal opportunities for all students, celebrate diversity, and constantly seek creative solutions to enhance mathematics education. Through these values, we aim to foster a culture of excellence and innovation in Rwanda's mathematical community.

OUR ACTIVITIES

RwMO activities

Camps & Ongoing Training

The Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad usually hosts both online and physical summer and winter camps that encourage open discussions and debates on various mathematical concepts and theorems. These camps foster a deeper understanding, enhance problem-solving skills, and aim to make math exciting and accessible.

Rwanda Math Competition

RwMO conducts both Junior and Senior Rwanda Mathematics Competition in Rwanda. These competitions are used to identify and select the most talented students to attend math camps and represent Rwanda in international mathematics competitions such as PAMO, IMO, and EAMO, etc.

International Math Competition

The Rwanda Mathematics Olympiad (RwMO) prepare the team Rwanda which participate in the international Math Competitions such as PAMO, IMO and EAMO and etc..

YEARY MAIN EVENTS

Rwanda Math Olympiad have 4 Main Yearly Events

1. RMC

The Rwanda Mathematics Competitions identify and nurture student talent through district, province, and national rounds, with Round 1 open to all high school students across Rwanda. Top performers gain recognition and are invited to participate in various Mathematics camps held in Rwanda, offering advanced training and opportunities for further development. These camps provide immersive learning experiences, deepening students' understanding of mathematical concepts and fostering connections with peers. Ultimately, this recognition and opportunity inspire students to excel in mathematics and contribute to Rwanda's growth in STEM fields.

2. EAMO

The East African Mathematics Olympiad (EAMO) is where the selected team from Rwanda competes alongside participants from other East African countries. This rigorous competition challenges both junior and senior students to solve intricate mathematical problems, fostering their problem-solving abilities and promoting excellence in mathematics. It serves as a platform to showcase Rwanda's talent and dedication to STEM education on a regional stage. .

3. PAMO

The Pan African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO) is where talented students from Rwanda join participants from across Africa in a challenging mathematical competition. Through PAMO, these students have the opportunity to showcase their problem-solving skills and mathematical prowess on a continental stage. PAMO not only fosters excellence in mathematics but also highlights Rwanda's commitment to STEM education and its place within the wider African mathematical community..

4. IMO

The International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) is where the best mathematical minds from Rwanda and around the world gather to compete in a prestigious annual competition. Representing their countries, students tackle exceptionally challenging mathematical problems over a series of days. IMO not only celebrates mathematical excellence but also fosters international cooperation and friendship among young mathematicians. For Rwanda, participation in IMO signifies its commitment to nurturing talent and promoting excellence in mathematics education on a global scale.

5. WITS AND KANGROO

In addition to EAMO, PAMO, and IMO, Rwanda also participates in various other prestigious international mathematics competitions such as Kangaroo, WITS, and other similar events. These competitions provide Rwandan students with valuable opportunities to showcase their mathematical talents and compete with peers from around the world. By participating in these events, Rwanda demonstrates its commitment to promoting excellence in mathematics education and nurturing the next generation of mathematical leaders..
