Rwanda National Mathematics Competiiton Registration

The Rwanda National Mathematics Competition is open to all high schools and students from S1 to S3. Schools are encouraged to register an equal number of male and female students to promote diversity in STEM fields. Each school must assign a coordinator to handle registration and communication.

Competition Format

  • Round 1: Held at the school level for all registered students.
  • Round 2: Regional competition for top performers from Round 1.
  • Round 3: The final round to select students for the National Mathematics Summer Camp.

Grading System

We will be using the CompetitionGradingBot for Round 1 and Round 2. Schools can test the grading system beforehand, you check how the bot works with the following video:

CompetitionGradingBot paper rules

Important Dates

  • Registration Deadline: To be announced
  • Round 1 Date: To be announced

Bank Account Information

In order to support teachers using our competitionGrading bot during Round 1 and Round 2, we request the their's bank account details. Teachers will be reimbursed the airtime to ensure smooth communication during these rounds. Additionally, each school will also reimbursed 5,000 FRW to cover expenses related to printing papers for the competition. Note: all of the reimbursment come after the competition.

Rewards

Top-performing students will receive certificates, medals, and a chance to join the Rwanda National Mathematics Team for international competitions, such as PAMO. They will also be invited to the Rwanda Mathematics Summer Camp.
